Smash Mirror

"mirror always lies; (souless surround) inside I die; deceit (placid facade), inside, I cry out;" ~ Mirror, zeroDecibel, lyrics aceXi3.


We often look at ourselves critically and expect others do, as well. And at times they do. At times they lie to us (as the mirror does), cheat us (as the mirror does) and knock us down (as the mirror does).


The anxiety that can build within us, can cripple us. But the mirror shows us a mere reflection. Not a reality. This is where we must believe beyond belief in ourselves - in our light - and connect only with those who give us life.
