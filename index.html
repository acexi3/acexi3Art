Featured Collection 01 :: MIRROR


We often look at ourselves critically and expect others do, as well. And at times they do. At times they lie to us (as the mirror does), cheat us (as the mirror does) and knock us down (as the mirror does).


Anxiety that builds within us can cripple us. But the mirror shows us mere reflection. Reflection of our image is trickery. This is why we must believe beyond belief - in ourselves, in our light - and connect only with those who give us life.
